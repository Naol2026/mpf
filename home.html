MODJO POULTRY FARM S.C

Nestled in Modjo town, just 73 kilometers south-east of Addis Ababa, lies Modjo Poultry Farm S.C. – a company dedicated to producing premium quality chicken and a diverse range of poultry products.



We're passionate about raising healthy chickens and providing our community with the finest poultry offerings available. Our commitment to excellence shines through in every product we create.

At Modjo Poultry Farm, we take pride in being a leader in the Ethiopian poultry industry. We are the only company in Ethiopia specifically established with the vision to export our exceptional poultry products to international markets, showcasing the quality and care that goes into everything we do.

We offer a delightful selection of poultry products, including:

• Fresh, Flavorful Chicken Eggs: Perfect for your family's breakfast or your next culinary creation.
• Tender and Succulent Chicken Meat: Raised with care and attention to ensure the highest quality and taste.
• Healthy Young Chickens: A strong foundation for raising your own flocks.

But our commitment extends beyond just providing excellent products. We are deeply invested in our community, striving to produce and sell our poultry at sustainable and affordable prices, making quality accessible to all.
